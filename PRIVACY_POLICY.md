# Privacy Policy for Rich Man

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Rich Man is a mobile app published under the package name `com.legendsoftware.richman`.

## Information We Collect

Rich Man does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, precise location, or other personal information.

The app stores coin balance information locally on the user's device so the app can show the current coin balance.

## Purchases

Rich Man uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. Rich Man receives purchase status information needed to unlock or add purchased coins, but payment details are handled by Google.

## Ads

Rich Man includes Google Mobile Ads SDK support. If ads are shown, Google may collect and process data according to Google's advertising and privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

Rich Man does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by Google services used by the app, including Google Play Billing and Google Mobile Ads, according to Google's policies.

## Children

Rich Man is not intended for children under 13.

## Security

Rich Man uses reasonable safeguards for app data stored on the device. No method of electronic storage is perfectly secure.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted in this file.

## Contact

For privacy questions about Rich Man, contact the app developer through the contact details listed on Google Play.
